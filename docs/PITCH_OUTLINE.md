# Pitch Outline (5–7 minutes)

1. **Problem** – What pain point are you solving? For whom?
2. **Solution** – Your product in one sentence and the key value prop.
3. **Demo walkthrough** – Show the happy path quickly; highlight the core feature.
4. **Architecture overview** – How frontend and backend collaborate; any integrations.
5. **Impact and future work** – Why it matters and what you would build next.
6. **Team organization** – Who did what; how you split work to ship fast.
